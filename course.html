Our Course Pathways

Choose from carefully designed programs that build academic confidence, digital skills, and life readiness.

Early Childhood Development (ECD)

Playful, nurturing supervision and early literacy support that inspire curiosity in young learners.

  • Child-centered learning activities
  • Language, music, and social play
  • Comfortable boarding options for young students

Primary Level (Grades 1–5)

Foundational knowledge in English, Nepali, math, science, and values to build an early academic edge.

  • Interactive worksheets and group projects
  • Emphasis on reading, writing, and reasoning
  • Moral education and school leadership opportunities

Secondary Level (Grades 6–10)

Board exam preparation combined with critical thinking, science labs, and English fluency.

  • Comprehensive revision and coaching
  • Project-based learning and exam practice
  • Preparation for future academic transitions

What Students Gain

Practical Learning
