Our Courses

From Early Childhood Development to Grade 10, we nurture every step of your child's learning journey.

Early Childhood Development (ECD)

  • Play-based, child-centered learning
  • Social & emotional growth
  • Cognitive skill development

Our ECD program lays a joyful foundation for lifelong learning, focusing on curiosity, creativity, and confidence.

Primary Level (Grades 1–5)

  • Core subjects: Language, Math, Science
  • Group activities & projects
  • Moral & value education

Building strong academic basics and social skills, our primary level inspires a love for learning and teamwork.

Secondary Level (Grades 6–10)

  • Board exam preparation
  • Critical thinking & problem-solving
  • Science & technology focus

Our secondary program prepares students for academic success and real-world challenges with advanced skills and confidence.
