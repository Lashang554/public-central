About Public Central School

Creating a safe, inclusive, and inspiring education experience for Letang's children since the school's foundation.

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality education that builds strong academic skills, confidence, and a sense of community in every student.

Our Vision

To become a trusted educational leader in Morang where innovation, empathy, and lifelong learning thrive.

Academic Programs

  • Early Childhood Development (ECD)
  • Primary Level (Grades 1–5)
  • Secondary Level (Grades 6–10)

Community Values

  • Respect for diversity across ethnic and cultural groups
  • Character education grounded in compassion and ethics
  • Strong family and local partnerships

Facilities

  • Modern classrooms and boarding spaces
  • Library, science lab, and playground
  • Support for digital literacy and extracurricular learning

Student Community

Public Central supports a welcoming student body from groups such as Limbu, Brahmin, Chhetri, Magar, Rai, Tamang, and more.

Location & Leadership

We are located in Letang Bhogtani, Morang, in the heart of a vibrant educational community.

School Guiding Principles

We believe that every learner deserves quality support, a safe campus, and the chance to succeed both academically and personally.

  • Personalized attention for each child
  • Structured exam preparation and career guidance
  • A curriculum rooted in national standards and local strengths
